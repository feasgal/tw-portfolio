# Deactivate a license


Return a license to your account so you can move it to another location.

This guide is for deactivating a license from a location connected to your machine. You don't need to deactivate licenses shared from a license server. Closing the protected app automatically releases the seat.

!!! goal clear-right

    Move a license from its current location back to your account.

## Before you start

Close any protected apps that might be using the license, then sign in to **Acme License Hub** so the change can complete cleanly.

## Steps

1. Find the license in its current location (Acme dongle or cloud session).
2. Choose **Deactivate**.
3. Confirm and wait for completion.

!!! outcome

    The license appears under **Available** in your account and can be activated elsewhere.
