# Transfer a license

Move a license from your account to another user’s account. This is useful for resale or handoff to a teammate.

!!! goal

    Send a license to another Acme account.

## Before you start

!!! warning inline end

    License transfers are irreversible.
    Make sure you have the correct Acme account for the recipient.

Confirm the product is eligible for transfer and understand any fees. Get the recipient’s **Acme account ID** and ensure the license is not activated to an Acme dongle or cloud session.

## Steps

1. In **Acme License Hub**, select the license in your account.
2. Choose **Transfer**.
3. Enter the recipient’s **Acme account ID**.
4. Review any fees and confirm the transfer.

!!! outcome

    The license leaves your account and appears in the recipient’s account, ready for activation.
